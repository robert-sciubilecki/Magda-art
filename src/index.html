BIO

Magdalena Mędzkiewicz

A Polish artist born in 1986, is best recognized for her abstract paintings and innovative contributions to contemporary art. Her creative journey began at the Academy of Fine Arts in Wrocław, where she delved into the world of Painting and Sculpture under the mentorship of Professor Janusz Merkel. With a dynamic career that spans various mediums, from street art and hydrophobic paintings to animation and monumental murals, Magdalena's canvas knows no bounds. Inspired by the urban landscapes of Wrocław and the transformative power of nature, Magdalena's works reflect her personal evolution. As an explorer of themes such as globalization and modern freedom harmonizing with the natural world, her art is an invitation to experience life through her vivid perspective. Magdalena Mędzkiewicz's portfolio encapsulates the essence of her artistic journey, inviting viewers to engage with her stories and expressions. As she continues to create from her base in Wrocław, her work resonates with those who seek to witness the confluence of creativity and life's myriad experiences.
